  <!-- Why -->
  <section id="why" class="section">
    <h2>Why a weekly plan helps</h2>
    <p class="lead"><span class="badge">Less effort, more order</span> Small, regular tasks prevent heavy “spring cleaning.” Pair work with good light, sturdy shoes, and breaks to protect joints and balance.</p>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Benefits</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Cleaner air and fewer dust/allergen flare-ups</li>
          <li>Lower fall risk from clutter and cords</li>
          <li>Predictable rhythm you can share with family or helpers</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        Use a <strong>timer (20–40 min)</strong>. Stop if you feel dizzy, short of breath, or have chest pain.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- ===== In-article AD ===== -->
  

  <!-- Kit -->
  <section id="kit" class="section">
    <h2>Starter kit (keep it all in one caddy)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Microfiber cloths (color-coded), extendable duster</li>
          <li>All-purpose cleaner + bathroom cleaner (clearly labeled)</li>
          <li>Dish soap, glass cleaner or vinegar mix, baking soda</li>
          <li>Disposable gloves, trash bags, small brush & dustpan</li>
          <li>Lightweight vacuum or broom, mop with wringer bucket</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card calm">
        Store <strong>off the floor</strong> at waist height to avoid bending. Keep Safety Data/labels readable (large print).
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Weekly plan -->
  <section id="weekly" class="section">
    <h2>Your 7-day plan (pick any start day)</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table" aria-label="Weekly housekeeping schedule">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Day</th><th scope="col">Task (20–40 min)</th><th scope="col">Quick checklist</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 1 — Kitchen Surfaces</strong></td>
          <td>Wipe counters, stove top, handles; empty old food</td>
          <td>Clear → spray → wipe; microwave 1-min steam; sink & drain</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 2 — Bathrooms</strong></td>
          <td>Toilet, sink, mirror, quick shower/tub rinse</td>
          <td>Cleaner on → 5-min soak → brush/rinse; replace towels</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 3 — Dust & High-touch</strong></td>
          <td>Dust from high to low; wipe switches/rails/remotes</td>
          <td>Extendable duster; damp cloth on flat surfaces</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 4 — Floors</strong></td>
          <td>Vacuum or sweep main paths; spot mop sticky areas</td>
          <td>Move small items only; mind cords; non-slip shoes</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 5 — Laundry & Linens</strong></td>
          <td>1–2 loads (clothes or linens); reset wardrobe</td>
          <td>Lights/darks; mesh bag for socks; change bed sheets</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 6 — Paper & Clutter</strong></td>
          <td>10-item toss/file; clear one surface (table/counter)</td>
          <td>Keep/Donate/Trash boxes ready; label and act</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Day 7 — Rest & Review</strong></td>
          <td>Walk-through: pick up, water plants, check supplies</td>
          <td>Refill caddy; list items to buy; celebrate wins</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
    <p class="small">Have help? Assign them the <em>lift/carry</em> tasks and anything requiring step stools.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- ===== In-article AD (after weekly) ===== -->
  

  <!-- 30-minute reset -->
  <section id="reset" class="section">
    <h2>30-minute weekend reset (timer on!)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">5-minute sweep</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Gather trash & recycling; empty small bins</li>
          <li>Return dishes to kitchen; start dishwasher or soak</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">10-minute kitchen</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Clear sink/counter; wipe stove & handles</li>
          <li>Check fridge “use-first” bin; plan 2 simple meals</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">10-minute bathroom</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Toilet bowl & seat; sink & mirror</li>
          <li>Swap towels; place fresh soap</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        <p class="kicker">5-minute floors</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Vacuum/sweep main path: entry → kitchen → bath</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Laundry -->
  <section id="laundry" class="section">
    <h2>Laundry rhythm (simple & gentle)</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table" aria-label="Laundry plan">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Item</th><th scope="col">How often</th><th scope="col">Tips</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Towels</strong></td>
          <td>Every 3–4 uses</td>
          <td>Dry fully between uses; avoid fabric softener on bath towels</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Bedsheets</strong></td>
          <td>Every 1–2 weeks</td>
          <td>Wash warm; dry completely to prevent musty smell</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Everyday clothes</strong></td>
          <td>When soiled or sweaty</td>
          <td>Mesh bag for socks/underwear; mild detergent</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Cleaning cloths</strong></td>
          <td>After a session</td>
          <td>Hot wash; keep a “dirty cloths” bag near caddy</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
  </section>

  <!-- Declutter -->
  <section id="declutter" class="section">
    <h2>Declutter in minutes (no drama)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">The 10-10-10</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Choose one zone. Remove 10 items: 5 trash, 3 donate, 2 keep elsewhere.</li>
          <li>Set a 10-minute timer; stop when it rings.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card calm">
        Keep a <strong>donation box</strong> near the door. When full, schedule pickup/drop-off.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Safe cleaning -->
  <section id="safe" class="section">
    <h2>Safe cleaning tips</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>No mixing</strong> bleach with ammonia/vinegar — dangerous fumes</li>
          <li>Ventilate: open a window or run the exhaust fan</li>
          <li>Label bottles in large print; keep in original containers</li>
          <li>Wear <strong>gloves</strong>; rinse skin if splashed</li>
          <li>Use step stools only with <strong>handrails</strong> — or ask for help</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card note">
        Pets/grandkids at home? Store chemicals up high or in a locked cabinet. Keep floors dry to prevent slips.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Falls & ergonomics -->
  <section id="falls" class="section">
    <h2>Fall-risk & ergonomics</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table" aria-label="Fall risk tips">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Do</th><th scope="col">Avoid</th><th scope="col">Why</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td>Wear closed-toe, non-slip shoes</td>
          <td>Cleaning in socks or sandals</td>
          <td>Better grip and toe protection</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td>Use long-handle tools; lift small loads</td>
          <td>Heavy buckets or twisting with vacuum</td>
          <td>Protects back and shoulders</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td>Keep cords against walls</td>
          <td>Loose cords across walkways</td>
          <td>Reduces trip hazards</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td>Hydrate and rest every 20–30 min</td>
          <td>“Pushing through” dizziness or pain</td>
          <td>Prevents overexertion and falls</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
    <p class="small">See also: <a href="/daily-living/bathroom-safety-setup">Bathroom Safety Setup</a> and <a href="/daily-living/lighting-for-aging-eyes">Lighting for Aging Eyes</a>.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- Tracking -->
  <section id="tracking" class="section">
    <h2>What to track</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Weekly tasks done (✓/✗) and time spent</li>
          <li>Supplies to reorder (cleaners, cloths, bags)</li>
          <li>Areas that felt unsafe or painful to reach</li>
          <li>Help needed (lift, ladder, deep clean) — schedule it</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        Take a quick photo of “problem spots” (cords, clutter). Use before/after pictures to stay motivated.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">How long should cleaning take each day?</p>
        <p>20–40 minutes is plenty for upkeep. Use the weekend reset for anything missed.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">I have arthritis—what helps?</p>
        <p>Lightweight tools, long handles, break tasks into 10-minute chunks, and avoid heavy lifting or wringing by hand.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Deep clean schedule?</p>
        <p>Quarterly: fridge shelves, oven, curtains, fans. Put one on the calendar each month so it never piles up.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Can I use vinegar with bleach?</p>
        <p>No. Never mix bleach with vinegar or ammonia—creates toxic gas. Use one or the other with water only.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
