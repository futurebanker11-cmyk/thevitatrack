  <!-- Steps -->
  <section id="steps" class="section">
    <h2>Your 5-step routine (30–45 minutes total)</h2>

    <div class="grid">
      <!-- Step 1 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">1</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Dim & time (5 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Choose a consistent bedtime; start wind-down 45–60 minutes before.</li>
          <li>Switch to warm lamps; lower room to a comfortable cool temperature.</li>
          <li>Set phones/TV to night mode; reduce volume and brightness.</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Aim for a steady sleep window even on weekends.</p>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 2 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">2</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Unload the mind (5–10 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Write “tomorrow list” (3 items max) and one line of gratitude.</li>
          <li>Send any must-do messages or set a reminder — then close the phone.</li>
          <li>Optional: 4–6 slow breaths (inhale through nose, exhale through pursed lips).</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 3 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">3</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Body care & gentle mobility (8–12 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Wash face/brush teeth; change into comfortable nightwear and supportive slippers.</li>
          <li>Seated stretches: ankle circles x10, calf stretch, shoulder rolls x6, easy neck look-left/right.</li>
          <li>If stiff joints: warm towel 5 minutes on the worst area.</li>
        </ul>
        <div class="ok">Keep movements painless and slow. Stop with chest pain, severe breathlessness, or dizziness.</div>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 4 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">4</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Smart fluids & medicines (5–10 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Shift most fluids earlier in the day; in the last 2–3 hours use small sips only.</li>
          <li>Take evening medicines as prescribed; prepare the morning pill box now.</li>
          <li>If you check evening glucose or use inhalers/CPAP, do it now.</li>
        </ul>
        <div class="info">On fluid restriction (heart/kidney), follow your daily limit. Ask about timing for diuretics to minimize night trips.</div>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 5 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">5</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Set the sleep environment (5–8 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Clear the path to the bathroom; switch on motion night lights.</li>
          <li>Use extra pillows or elevate head/upper chest if reflux or breathlessness.</li>
          <li>Cool, dark, and quiet — or soft fan/white noise if helpful.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Adjustments -->
  <section id="adjust" class="section">
    <h2>Adjustments by condition</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Condition</th><th scope="col">Adjustments</th><th scope="col">Watch-outs</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Insomnia</strong></td>
          <td>Anchor wake-time; use the routine nightly; bed only when sleepy; if awake &gt;20 min, get up to read quietly under dim light.</td>
          <td>Avoid long daytime naps; skip late caffeine (after ~6 pm).</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>GERD / reflux</strong></td>
          <td>Finish dinner 3+ hours before bed; elevate head/upper chest with wedge or extra pillows; choose low-acid evening snacks.</td>
          <td>Avoid large/fatty/spicy meals late; no lying flat after eating.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Nocturia / BPH</strong></td>
          <td>Limit fluids 2–3 hours pre-bed; double-void before lights out; keep a clear, lit path to the bathroom.</td>
          <td>Dizziness on standing — rise slowly, consider a bedside commode if needed.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Type 2 diabetes</strong></td>
          <td>If on insulin/sulfonylureas and prone to lows: small protein-carb snack per plan; check glucose at bedtime as advised.</td>
          <td>Shaking/sweating/odd dreams — treat lows per your clinician’s instructions.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Chronic pain / arthritis</strong></td>
          <td>Warm shower or heating pad (on low) 10–15 min before bed; pillow between knees or under calves to unload joints.</td>
          <td>Avoid falling asleep with a hot pad on high; protect skin.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>COPD / heart failure</strong></td>
          <td>Use inhalers/neb/CPAP/BiPAP as prescribed; elevate head; keep rescue inhaler nearby.</td>
          <td>Sudden breathlessness, chest pain, or new swelling — seek care.</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
  </section>

  <!-- Foods & drinks -->
  <section id="foods" class="section">
    <h2>Evening food & drinks</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Good picks (light & steady)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Warm milk or chamomile; small yogurt with berries; banana with peanut butter; oats or khichdi (small bowl).</li>
          <li>Hydrate earlier in the evening; switch to small sips later.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Skip late if possible</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Large or spicy/fatty meals; chocolate, cola, strong tea/coffee late.</li>
          <li>Alcohol as a “sleep aid” — it fragments sleep and worsens reflux.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        If you wake hungry at night, keep a small planned snack at the bedside to avoid rummaging in the dark.
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        Ask your clinician which medicines should be taken with food vs on an empty stomach at night.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Night safety -->
  <section id="safety" class="section">
    <h2>Night-safety setup</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Night lights with motion sensors from bed to bathroom.</li>
          <li>Clear rugs/clutter; secure cords; consider grab bars.</li>
          <li>Stable chair near the bed for a brief sit if you feel dizzy.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card note">
        Keep phone, glasses, water, and a list of medicines on the bedside table. If you use hearing aids, place them where they won’t fall.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
