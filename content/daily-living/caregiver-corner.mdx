  <section id="daily-plan" class="section">
    <h2>Daily care plan (AM / PM)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Morning</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Vitals if advised (BP, glucose, weight for CHF/CKD).</li>
          <li>Medicines with a simple checkmark routine.</li>
          <li>Breakfast: fiber + protein; hydrate.</li>
          <li>Movement: 5–10 minute walk or balance set.</li>
          <li>Hygiene: teeth, skin moisturizer, hearing aids/eyeglasses on.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Evening</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Set pill box for tomorrow; confirm refills.</li>
          <li>Gentle stretch; bathroom safety lights on.</li>
          <li>Wind-down: screens off 60 minutes; bedroom cool and dark.</li>
          <li>Night bathroom plan: clear path, non-slip socks, grab bars.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

    <h2>Appointment prep & questions</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Pack this</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Binder (meds list, diagnoses, allergies, recent vitals).</li>
          <li>Pill box or photos of labels.</li>
          <li>Questions list (top 3 first).</li>

      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Smart questions</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>What is the <strong>goal</strong> of this medicine/test?</li>
          <li>What side-effects should we watch for?</li>
          <li>What <strong>one thing</strong> can we do daily to help?</li>
          <li>When should we <strong>call</strong> you vs visit ER?</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">High-impact fixes</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Clear floors; remove loose rugs; add <strong>grab bars</strong> & non-slip mats.</li>
          <li>Night lights: bed → bathroom path.</li>
          <li>Sturdy shoes; avoid long trailing clothes.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Kitchen & meds</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Store heavy items at waist height.</li>
          <li>Label look-alike bottles clearly; lock duplicates.</li>
          <li>Keep a <strong>hydration station</strong> visible.</li>
        </ul>

  <!-- Behavior & communication -->
  <section id="behavior" class="section">
    <h2>Behavior & communication tips</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Use <strong>short steps</strong>: one instruction at a time.</li>
          <li>Calm voice, eye contact, gentle touch if welcome.</li>
          <li>Offer 2 choices, not open-ended questions.</li>
          <li>Change the <strong>environment</strong> first (light, noise, temperature) before adding medicines for behavior.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        New confusion, agitation, or sleepy spells can mean infection, dehydration, medicine side-effect, or blood sugar shifts. Seek timely assessment.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

    <div class="grid">

          <li>Doctor names & numbers; preferred hospital.</li>

      </div>

          <li>Phone charger, small cash, list of questions.</li>
        </ul>

  <section id="respite" class="section">
    <h2>Respite & support</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Protect your energy</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Plan <strong>two short breaks</strong> daily (walk, tea, call a friend).</li>
          <li>Rotate tasks with family or neighbors when possible.</li>
          <li>Ask your clinician about <strong>home health</strong> or therapy referrals.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card note">
        Caregiver stress is real. If sleep, mood, or health is slipping, tell your clinician — help exists.

  <!-- Red flags -->
  <section id="redflags" class="section">
    <h2>When to call for help</h2>
    <div class="danger">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>New <strong>chest pain</strong>, severe breathlessness, fainting.</li>
        <li>Sudden confusion, weakness on one side, slurred speech.</li>
        <li>High fever, severe dehydration (very sleepy, no urine), or black/bloody stool.</li>
        <li>Falls with head hit or blood thinners.</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="small">Call your local emergency number when in doubt.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers (FAQ)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">How do I organize everything fast?</p>
        <p>Use one binder + one calendar. Start with the medicines list and next three appointments.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">What if my loved one refuses care?</p>
        <p>Try at a calmer time, offer two choices, and link tasks to their goals (“so you can walk in the garden”). Ask your clinician about strategies if it continues.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">How can I prevent burnout?</p>
        <p>Schedule daily micro-breaks, share tasks, and say yes to respite offers. If overwhelmed, tell your clinician — it’s part of the care plan.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Which vitals should I track?</p>
        <p>Depends on conditions: BP, weight (CHF/CKD), glucose (diabetes), temperature with illness. Bring logs to visits.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
