    <h2>Why a morning routine helps</h2>
    <p class="lead"><span class="kicker">Benefits:</span> steadier blood pressure when standing, fewer falls, better blood sugar control, less morning stiffness, and a calm start that sets the tone for the day.</p>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">What you’ll need</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Stable chair with arms and good shoes by the bed.</li>
          <li>Water bottle (room temperature), pill organizer, small snack.</li>
          <li>Notebook/phone for a 1-minute plan and symptom notes.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        Keep paths clear, use night lights, and place often-used items at waist height to avoid bending or reaching.
      </div>
    </div>

  <!-- Steps -->
  <section id="steps" class="section">
    <h2>Your 5-step routine (10–15 minutes)</h2>

    <div class="grid">
      <!-- Step 1 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">1</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Wake & check-in (1–2 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Before standing: sit up, feet on the floor, <strong>3 slow breaths</strong>.</li>
          <li>Note how you feel: dizziness, chest discomfort, unusual shortness of breath, severe headache? If yes, pause and reassess.</li>
          <li>Look at your environment: lights on, path clear, non-slip footwear on.</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">If one-sided weakness, slurred speech, or new vision trouble — seek urgent care.</p>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 2 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">2</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Hydrate & medicines (2–3 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Small sips of water</strong> (½–1 cup). Avoid chugging to reduce light-headedness.</li>
          <li>Take morning medicines as prescribed; use the pill box to prevent missed doses.</li>
          <li>If a medicine needs food, take it in Step 5 with breakfast.</li>
        </ul>
        <div class="info">If you’re on fluid restriction (e.g., heart/kidney disease), follow your clinician’s daily total.</div>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 3 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">3</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Gentle mobility (3–5 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>From a sturdy chair: <strong>10 ankle pumps</strong> each side → <strong>5 sit-to-stands</strong> (use arms as needed).</li>
          <li>Posture reset: chin gently back, shoulder rolls x5, slow neck look-left/right.</li>
          <li>If steady, walk the hallway for 2 minutes or around the room.</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Stop with chest pain, severe breathlessness, or dizziness.</p>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 4 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">4</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Bathroom & safety (2–3 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Use grab bars if available; move slowly from sitting → standing.</li>
          <li>Teeth, face wash, and hands — a quick refresh increases alertness.</li>
          <li>If you check your morning weight (heart/kidney care), do it now and write it down.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <!-- Step 5 -->
      <article class="card step">
        <div class="num">5</div>
        <h3 class="kicker">Breakfast & plan (3–4 min)</h3>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Protein + fiber:</strong> eggs or yogurt + fruit/oats; or dal/chilla; whole-grain toast.</li>
          <li>Take medicines that require food. Set a reminder for mid-day and evening doses.</li>
          <li>Make a 1-minute plan: one movement goal (walk, stretches) and one small task.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Adjustments by condition -->
  <section id="adjust" class="section">
    <h2>Adjustments by condition</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Condition</th><th scope="col">Adjustments</th><th scope="col">Watch-outs</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>High blood pressure / dizziness on standing</strong></td>
          <td>Extra 30–60 sec sitting; ankle pumps before standing; rise slowly; take BP after 5 min seated if needed.</td>
          <td>Very low readings with symptoms → call clinician.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Type 2 diabetes</strong></td>
          <td>Do not delay breakfast if on insulin/sulfonylureas; carry quick carbs. Small walk after eating helps glucose.</td>
          <td>Shaking/sweating/confusion = check glucose and treat lows per plan.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Arthritis / joint pain</strong></td>
          <td>Warm towel on stiff joints 5 min; shorten sit-to-stands, add gentle range-of-motion.</td>
          <td>Avoid painful ranges; use supportive shoes.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Chronic kidney disease / heart failure</strong></td>
          <td>Fluids per plan; weigh daily after bathroom; note leg swelling/breathlessness.</td>
          <td>Sudden weight ↑ (≥1–2 kg in 2–3 days) → call clinician.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Reflux (GERD)</strong></td>
          <td>Breakfast choices: low-acid fruit, oats, yogurt; avoid lying down after meals.</td>
          <td>Skip very spicy/fatty breakfasts if they trigger symptoms.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Balance issues / prior falls</strong></td>
          <td>Keep a walker/cane by the bed; turn on lights first; consider a seated warm-up longer (2–3 min).</td>
          <td>Avoid rushing to the bathroom; clear rugs/clutter.</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
  </section>

  <!-- Home monitoring -->
  <section id="vitals" class="section">
    <h2>If you monitor at home</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Blood pressure</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Measure after 5 minutes seated, back supported, arm at heart level.</li>
          <li>Optional: check standing at 1 minute if dizziness occurs.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Glucose</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Check fasting if advised; treat lows per your clinician’s plan before moving around.</li>
          <li>A short walk after breakfast supports glucose control.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Weight & symptoms</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Record morning weight (same scale/time/clothes) if you have heart/kidney conditions.</li>
          <li>Note breathlessness, swelling, chest discomfort, or dizziness.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card note">
        Share your log during appointments. It speeds adjustments to medicines and goals.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Night-before -->
  <section id="setup" class="section">
    <h2>Night-before setup (2 minutes)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Place water, glasses, phone, and pill organizer on the bedside table.</li>
          <li>Lay out shoes with non-slip soles; clear a walking path; switch on a night light.</li>
          <li>Write tomorrow’s one small task and one movement goal.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        Consistency beats perfection — even 3 steps completed most days helps.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
