  <!-- What is -->
  <section id="what-is-covid" class="section">
    <h2>What is COVID-19?</h2>
    <p class="lead"><span class="badge">In short</span> A respiratory infection that can affect the lungs, heart, brain, and other organs. Older adults and people with conditions like heart, lung, kidney disease, diabetes, or weak immunity have higher risk of severe illness.</p>
    <div class="card small">
      <p><strong>Good to know:</strong> Early testing and prompt contact with your clinician can open the door to <em>antiviral treatment</em> in the first days of symptoms. Keep a current medicine list handy.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Red flags -->
  <section id="red-flags" class="section">
    <h2>When to seek urgent help</h2>
    <div class="danger">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Struggling to breathe, blue/gray lips or face, cannot speak full sentences</li>
        <li>New confusion, fainting, or severe chest pain</li>
        <li>Very low oxygen reading on your usual home device or a rapid drop from your normal</li>
        <li>Severe dehydration (very little urine, dizziness on standing)</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="small">These can be emergencies — seek care immediately.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Symptoms -->
  <section id="symptoms" class="section">
    <h2>Common symptoms</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Fever or chills, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose</li>
          <li>Cough, shortness of breath, chest tightness</li>
          <li>Headache, body aches, fatigue</li>
          <li>Loss or change of taste/smell (may or may not occur)</li>
          <li>Upset stomach: nausea, vomiting, or diarrhea</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Higher-risk situations</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Age 65+, or multiple medical conditions</li>
          <li>Immunosuppression (medicines or conditions)</li>
          <li>Recently hospitalized for heart or lung issues</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Testing & isolation -->
  <section id="testing" class="section">
    <h2>Testing & isolation basics</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">When to test</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Symptoms present or known exposure — use a rapid antigen test now and repeat if the first is negative while symptoms continue.</li>
          <li>Before visiting someone high-risk, consider testing the same day.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">If positive</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Stay home away from others as advised by your clinician/local health guidance.</li>
          <li>Improve air: open windows, use exhaust fans or air purifiers.</li>
          <li>Wear a high-filtration mask if you must be around others.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
    <p class="small info" style="margin-top:10px">Details can change. Follow your local public health guidance and your clinician’s advice.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- Treatment -->
  <section id="treatment" class="section">
    <h2>Treatment timing & interactions</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Act early</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Contact your clinician as soon as symptoms start. Antiviral pills are generally started in the first few days of illness for those at higher risk.</li>
          <li>Bring your <strong>full medicine list</strong> (including supplements). Some antivirals interact with common heart or cholesterol medicines; your dose may be adjusted or a different treatment chosen.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Supportive care</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Fluids, rest, and nutrition</li>
          <li>Fever/aches: acetaminophen as directed (mind total daily dose)</li>
          <li>Cough relief and nasal saline if helpful</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
    <p class="small note" style="margin-top:10px">Treatment is individualized. Kidney, liver, and heart conditions — and your current medicines — guide the safest plan.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- Home care -->
  <section id="home-care" class="section">
    <h2>Home care & monitoring</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Daily checks</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Temperature, symptoms, and fluid intake</li>
          <li>If you use a pulse oximeter: note your usual level and alert your clinician if it drops or stays low for you</li>
          <li>Keep medicines organized; refill reminders help</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Protect household</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Ventilate rooms; consider a HEPA air cleaner in shared spaces</li>
          <li>Mask when near others; separate sleeping if possible</li>
          <li>Clean hands, high-touch surfaces, and shared devices</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Prevention -->
  <section id="prevention" class="section">
    <h2>Prevention: air, masks, vaccines</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Better air</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Open windows and cross-ventilate when visitors come by</li>
          <li>Use exhaust fans (kitchen/bath) or a portable air cleaner</li>
          <li>Meet outdoors when practical</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Masks & shots</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Wear a high-filtration mask (well-fitted) in crowded indoor spaces</li>
          <li>Stay “up to date” with COVID-19 vaccination per local guidance</li>
          <li>Ask whether you’re eligible for additional protection based on your health</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Travel -->
  <section id="travel" class="section">
    <h2>Travel & visiting others</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Before you go</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Pack a medicine list and extra doses</li>
          <li>Carry a few rapid tests and high-filtration masks</li>
          <li>Plan for fresh-air breaks; choose less-crowded times when possible</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card calm">
        Visiting grandkids? Meet outdoors or in well-ventilated rooms; consider quick same-day testing if someone recently had symptoms.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Long COVID -->
  <section id="long-covid" class="section">
    <h2>Long COVID overview</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Common issues</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Fatigue, shortness of breath, brain fog, sleep changes, mood symptoms</li>
          <li>Symptoms can improve gradually; pacing helps avoid crashes</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Follow-up</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Keep a symptom and activity log</li>
          <li>Ask about pulmonary rehab, sleep evaluation, and mood support if needed</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Questions -->
  <section id="questions" class="section">
    <h2>Questions for your clinician</h2>
    <div class="card">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Am I eligible for antiviral treatment if I test positive? How soon should I start?</li>
        <li>Do any of my medicines interact with COVID treatments?</li>
        <li>What should my isolation and masking plan look like at home?</li>
        <li>Which vaccines or boosters are recommended for me now?</li>
        <li>When should I check oxygen levels or seek in-person care?</li>
        <li>How do we evaluate lingering symptoms after recovery?</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">When should I contact my clinician?</p>
        <p>Right away if you’re high-risk and develop symptoms — early treatment windows are short.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Do I still need to test?</p>
        <p>Yes, when you have symptoms or a known exposure. Repeat if the first antigen test is negative and symptoms continue.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Which mask works best?</p>
        <p>A well-fitted, high-filtration mask (e.g., respirator-style) offers better protection in crowded indoor spaces.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">What about medicine interactions?</p>
        <p>Some antivirals interact with common heart or cholesterol medicines. Share your full medicine list so your clinician can choose safely.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
