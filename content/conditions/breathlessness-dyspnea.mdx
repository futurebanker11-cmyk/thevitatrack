  <!-- What is -->
  <section id="what-is-dyspnea" class="section">
    <h2>What is breathlessness?</h2>
    <p class="lead"><span class="badge">In short</span> Breathlessness (dyspnea) is the uncomfortable feeling of not getting enough air. It can appear during activity, when lying flat, or suddenly at rest. The most common causes in older adults are lung issues, heart problems, low blood count, and deconditioning.</p>
    <div class="card small">
      <p><strong>Key idea:</strong> Finding the cause guides the fix — from inhalers or fluid pills to iron or breathing rehab. Seek urgent help for red-flags below.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Red flags -->
  <section id="red-flags" class="section">
    <h2>When to seek urgent help</h2>
    <div class="danger">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Severe shortness of breath at rest, gasping, bluish lips</li>
        <li>Chest pain/pressure, sweating, or pain spreading to arm/jaw</li>
        <li>Sudden breathlessness with one-sided leg swelling (possible clot)</li>
        <li>New confusion, fainting, or very low oxygen if known</li>
        <li>Wheezing with fast breathing not improving with usual inhaler</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="small">Call local emergency services right away for these symptoms.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Symptoms & clues -->
  <section id="symptoms" class="section">
    <h2>Common symptoms & clues</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Out of breath on stairs or walking room-to-room</li>
          <li>Needing extra pillows to breathe when lying down</li>
          <li>Night waking short of breath (paroxysmal nocturnal dyspnea)</li>
          <li>Wheeze, cough with phlegm, or chest tightness</li>
          <li>Ankle swelling, fast heartbeat, fatigue, or pale skin</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Related topics</p>

          <li>Hydration & Drinks</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

  </section>

  <section id="causes" class="section">
    <h2>Common causes (heart, lungs, others)</h2>
    <table class="table" role="table">
      <thead>
        <tr><th scope="col">Category</th><th scope="col">Examples</th><th scope="col">Clues</th></tr>
      </thead>
      <tbody>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Lungs</strong></td>
          <td>Asthma/COPD flare, pneumonia, COVID, pulmonary embolism, interstitial lung disease</td>
          <td>Wheeze or crackles, cough/fever, chest tightness, sudden onset with chest pain</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Heart</strong></td>
          <td>Heart failure, ischemia/heart attack, abnormal rhythm</td>
          <td>Orthopnea, ankle swelling, rapid weight gain, chest pressure, palpitations</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Blood</strong></td>
          <td>Anemia (low hemoglobin)</td>
          <td>Pale, fatigue, dizzy on exertion; low CBC hemoglobin</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
          <td><strong>Other</strong></td>
          <td>Anxiety/panic, deconditioning, obesity, thyroid problems, acid reflux with micro-aspiration</td>
          <td>Normal tests but symptoms with stress, recent inactivity, snoring/reflux</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>

  <!-- Assessment -->
  <section id="assessment" class="section">
    <h2>How clinicians evaluate dyspnea</h2>
    <div class="card">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>History & exam (onset, triggers, position, cough/fever, chest pain, swelling, snoring/reflux)</li>
        <li>Vitals & oxygen level; heart and lung sounds</li>
        <li>Tests as needed: <strong>CBC</strong>, metabolic panel, <strong>chest X-ray</strong>, <strong>ECG</strong>, BNP (heart strain), spirometry/peak flow, pulse oximetry</li>
        <li>Further tests when indicated: echocardiogram, CT scan for clots, thyroid tests, sleep study</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="small">Testing depends on your story and exam — not everyone needs every test.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Treatment -->
  <section id="treatment" class="section">
    <h2>Treatment options (by cause)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Lung flare (asthma/COPD)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Rescue inhaler as directed; controller inhalers for prevention</li>
          <li>Short courses of steroids/antibiotics only when indicated</li>
          <li>Pulmonary rehab and vaccines (flu, pneumonia, COVID as advised)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Heart failure or fluid overload</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Diuretics (fluid tablets), salt/fluid guidance, weight monitoring</li>
          <li>Heart-protective medicines as prescribed; device therapy in selected cases</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Anemia</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Treat the cause (iron/B12/folate deficiency, bleeding); iron or B12 as directed</li>
          <li>Recheck blood counts to confirm recovery</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Anxiety or deconditioning</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Breathing practice, graded activity, relaxation skills</li>
          <li>Address sleep & reflux; consider therapy support if panic symptoms</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
    <p class="small note" style="margin-top:12px">Treatment is individualized. Follow your clinician’s plan and action steps for worsening symptoms.</p>
  </section>

  <!-- Techniques -->
  <section id="techniques" class="section">
    <h2>Breathing techniques that help</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Pursed-lip breathing</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Inhale through the nose for 2 counts</li>
          <li>Exhale gently through pursed lips for 4 counts (like blowing out a candle)</li>
          <li>Use during activity or when breathless to slow breathing and improve comfort</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Positions of ease</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Tripod: sit leaning forward with hands on thighs</li>
          <li>High side-lying: pillows behind back and between knees</li>
          <li>Sleep with head elevated if breathing worsens when flat</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Daily care -->
  <section id="daily-care" class="section">
    <h2>Daily care & energy tips</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Smart pacing</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Break tasks into steps; rest before you’re exhausted</li>
          <li>Exhale on effort (e.g., when standing) to ease strain</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Hydration & salt (as advised)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Steady daytime fluids unless restricted; avoid heavy evening loads</li>
          <li>Follow salt guidance for heart/lung conditions</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Environment & triggers</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Avoid smoke, strong fumes, and dusty areas</li>
          <li>Use masks or air filters during poor air quality days</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card calm">
        If symptoms suddenly worsen, follow your action plan and seek care promptly.
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="small" style="margin-top:8px">
      Related: Asthma & COPD · <a href="/conditions/💗-heart-failure-basics">Heart Failure</a> · <a href="/conditions/anemia">Anemia</a> · Hydration & Drinks
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Questions -->
  <section id="questions" class="section">
    <h2>Questions for your clinician</h2>
    <div class="card">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>What seems to be the main cause of my breathlessness?</li>
        <li>Which tests are necessary now vs. later?</li>
        <li>Do I need an inhaler, fluid tablet, iron/B12, or other treatment?</li>
        <li>What’s my home action plan if symptoms flare?</li>
        <li>Which exercises or rehab programs fit me best?</li>
        <li>Which vaccines, devices (spacers, oximeter), or monitors should I use?</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Can anxiety cause breathlessness?</p>
        <p>Yes. It can change breathing pattern and chest muscle tension. Techniques and reassurance often help alongside medical evaluation.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Why worse at night or lying flat?</p>
        <p>Fluid shifts and airway mechanics can worsen symptoms when flat — common in heart failure and some lung conditions.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Is pulse oximeter useful?</p>
        <p>It can help track trends but doesn’t replace medical advice. Seek care for persistent low readings or severe symptoms.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Do I need oxygen?</p>
        <p>Only if tests show low oxygen at rest or with activity. Many breathlessness causes improve without home oxygen.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
