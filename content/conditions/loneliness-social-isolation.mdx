  <section id="what-is" class="section">
    <h2>What it is & why it matters</h2>
    <p class="lead"><span class="badge">In short</span> <strong>Loneliness</strong> is feeling alone or disconnected. <strong>Social isolation</strong> means having few social contacts. Both can affect mood, sleep, thinking, heart health, and independence—but small, steady steps can make a real difference.</p>
    <div class="card small">
      <p><strong>Good to know:</strong> Even one reliable connection (a neighbor, family member, faith group, club) can reduce stress and lift energy. Quality matters more than quantity.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Red flags -->
  <section id="red-flags" class="section">
    <h2>When to get urgent help</h2>
    <div class="danger">
      <ul class="list">
        <li>Thoughts of harming yourself or not wanting to live</li>
        <li>Severe depression with inability to eat, drink, or get out of bed</li>
        <li>Confusion, new disorientation, or sudden behavior change</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="small">If you or someone you know is in immediate danger: call your local emergency number now. If you’re having thoughts of self-harm, contact your country’s crisis line or speak to a clinician right away.</p>
    </div>
  </section>

    <h2>Common signs</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Withdrawing from activities you used to enjoy</li>
          <li>Sleep problems; low energy; appetite change</li>
          <li>Feeling like “a burden,” emptiness, or hopelessness</li>
          <li>More TV/phone time but fewer meaningful chats</li>
          <li>Missing appointments, reduced self-care, clutter building up</li>
        </ul>

  <!-- Screen -->
  <section id="screen" class="section">
    <h2>Simple self-check</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">3 quick questions (past 2 weeks)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>How often do you feel left out or isolated?</li>
          <li>How often do you lack companionship?</li>
          <li>How satisfied are you with your social contacts?</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Frequent “often” or “always” responses suggest it’s time to build a connection plan and talk to a clinician if mood is low.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        <strong>Hearing & vision first:</strong> Addressing these often unlocks social engagement. A simple earwax check or updated glasses can make calls and outings easier.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Daily plan -->
  <section id="daily-plan" class="section">
    <h2>Daily connection plan (7-day)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Core habits (everyday)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>1 meaningful contact</strong> (5–10 min): call, voice note, or doorstep chat</li>
          <li><strong>Light movement</strong> outdoors if possible (10–20 min)</li>
          <li><strong>Small act</strong> for someone: text, share an article, check on a neighbor</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Weekly anchors</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Choose <strong>2 recurring</strong> activities (club, worship, class, walking group)</li>
          <li>Put them on a visible calendar; arrange transport or a buddy</li>
          <li>Try one <strong>volunteer micro-task</strong> (phone check-in program, library help)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="note" style="margin-top:12px">Small steps count. Consistency over perfection.</div>
  </section>

  <!-- Conversation starters -->
  <section id="convos" class="section">
    <h2>Conversation starters</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Call/text ideas</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>“Thought of you when I saw this—how are you today?”</li>
          <li>“Want to try a 10-minute walk together this week?”</li>
          <li>“What are you cooking tonight? I’m trying ___.”</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Group prompts</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Share a photo from the week and one highlight</li>
          <li>Swap a favorite song, book, or recipe; try it by next chat</li>
          <li>Set a shared step goal or puzzle-of-the-week</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Tech -->
  <section id="tech" class="section">
    <h2>Safe tech for staying in touch</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Make it easy</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Use a <strong>large-text phone/tablet</strong> with <em>Favorites</em> on the home screen</li>
          <li>One-button video call shortcuts; label with names/photos</li>
          <li>Smart speaker: “Call Anita” or “Remind me to ring Raj at 5 pm”</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        <strong>Stay safe online:</strong> Don’t send money or personal details in chats. If something feels off, check with a family member first.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

          <li>Community transport and walking groups</li>
          <li>Phone befriending programs and volunteer hotlines</li>

  <!-- Caregivers -->
  <section id="caregivers" class="section">
    <h2>For caregivers</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Spot the clues</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Less talking, missed calls, or “I don’t want to bother anyone”</li>
          <li>Decline in housekeeping or personal grooming</li>
          <li>New irritability, sadness, or sleep reversal (awake at night)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Make connection easy</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Set two weekly recurring visits/calls with family or neighbors</li>
          <li>Arrange transport; share a calendar on the fridge and phone</li>
          <li>Pair medical visits with a pleasant stop (tea, park bench chat)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Is living alone the same as being lonely?</p>
        <p>No. Some people who live alone feel well-connected; others feel lonely in a crowd. The goal is meaningful contact, not just more people.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Does loneliness affect health?</p>
        <p>Yes—linked to low mood, poor sleep, higher stress, and reduced activity. Improving connection often improves overall health behaviors.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">I’m shy—what’s one low-pressure step?</p>
        <p>Send a short voice note or text to one person today. Timed weekly calls or a small group activity can build comfort gradually.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">When should I talk to a clinician?</p>
        <p>If low mood, anxiety, or sleep problems persist; if appetite/weight changes; or if you’ve lost interest in usual activities.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
