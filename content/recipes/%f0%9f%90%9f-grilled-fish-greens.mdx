  <section id="ingredients" class="section">
    <h2>Ingredients & portions</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Fish (2 servings)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Fish fillets:</strong> 2 × 120–150 g (salmon, tilapia, rohu boneless, pomfret steak)</li>
          <li><strong>Olive oil:</strong> 2 tsp</li>
          <li><strong>Lemon:</strong> zest + juice (½ lemon), extra wedges</li>
          <li><strong>Seasoning:</strong> black pepper or paprika pinch (no added salt)</li>
          <li><strong>Herbs:</strong> dill/parsley/coriander (small handful)</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Use boneless fillets if chewing is difficult. Fish should smell clean and fresh.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Greens side</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Leafy greens:</strong> 4 cups loosely packed (spinach/amaranth/methi or a mix)</li>
          <li><strong>Olive oil:</strong> 1 tsp</li>
          <li><strong>Garlic powder</strong> pinch (not garlic salt)</li>
          <li><strong>Lemon</strong> squeeze to finish</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Spinach cooks down significantly; wash well and dry before sautéing.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Steps: grill / tawa -->
  <section id="steps" class="section">
    <h2>Grill pan / tawa steps</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ol class="list">
          <li><strong>Preheat:</strong> Heat a grill pan or tawa on medium. Lightly oil with ½ tsp olive oil.</li>
          <li><strong>Prep fish:</strong> Pat dry. Rub with remaining oil, lemon zest, pepper/paprika, and herbs.</li>
          <li><strong>Cook:</strong> Place fish on the hot pan. Cook 3–5 min per side (thin tilapia) or 4–6 min (thicker salmon/pomfret) until opaque and flakes easily.</li>
          <li><strong>Finish:</strong> Rest 2 minutes. Squeeze fresh lemon just before serving.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        <strong>Moist texture tip:</strong> Cover loosely with a lid for the last minute to trap steam and keep the fish tender.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Greens -->
  <section id="greens" class="section">
    <h2>Warm greens (quick sauté)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ol class="list">
          <li>Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a wide pan on medium-low.</li>
          <li>Add washed, dried greens. Toss gently 1–3 minutes until wilted (spinach) or just tender (methi/amaranth).</li>
          <li>Season with pepper and a squeeze of lemon. No salt needed.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="card note">
        <strong>Swallowing ease:</strong> Chop greens finely or cook slightly longer with a spoon of water for a softer bite.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Condition swaps -->
  <section id="condition-swaps" class="section">
    <h2>Swaps by condition</h2>
    <div class="three">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Diabetes (Type 2)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Balance your plate: ½ non-starchy greens, ¼ fish, ¼ whole grain (brown rice/millet ½ cup cooked).</li>
          <li>Avoid sugary glazes; lemon and herbs add flavor without carbs.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Heart & BP</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>No added salt.</strong> Use pepper, lemon, and herbs for brightness.</li>
          <li>Choose salmon more often for omega-3s; keep total oil to ~3 tsp for the meal.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">CKD (Kidney)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Portion fish ~120 g cooked (per plan). Avoid salty seasoning mixes.</li>
          <li>Choose lower-potassium greens (spinach in small portions after cooking) or mix with beans/carrots.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Reflux (GERD)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Use mild spice only. Avoid late, large dinners; keep lemon moderate if sensitive.</li>
          <li>Grill/tawa is fine; avoid heavy frying.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Chewing/swallowing</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Pick boneless fillets and check for pin bones.</li>
          <li>Flake fish and serve with soft greens or mashed veggies.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Safety -->
  <section id="safety" class="section">
    <h2>Safety & doneness</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Internal temp:</strong> ~63°C / 145°F at thickest part or until flakes easily and is opaque.</li>
          <li><strong>Bones:</strong> Slide a finger along fillet to feel for pins and remove before cooking.</li>
          <li><strong>Storage:</strong> Refrigerate within 2 hours; use within 1–2 days. Reheat gently.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card danger">
        <strong>Allergy:</strong> If fish allergy is suspected, do not prepare. Discuss alternatives with your clinician.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers (FAQ)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">No grill pan?</p>
        <p>Use a non-stick tawa. Cook on medium with a thin oil layer. Flip once; cover briefly to steam-finish.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Skin on or off?</p>
        <p>Either. Skin-on keeps fish moist; remove after cooking if preferred.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Best herbs?</p>
        <p>Dill/parsley for salmon; coriander for tilapia/rohu. Avoid salty mixes.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">What grain pairs well?</p>
        <p>Brown rice, millet, or quinoa (½ cup cooked). Keep portions modest for glucose and weight goals.</p>
      </div>
