  <section id="ingredients" class="section">
    <h2>Ingredients (base + options)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Base (about 5 cups total)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>1½ cups <strong>unsalted nuts</strong> (almonds, walnuts, cashews, peanuts)</li>
          <li>1 cup <strong>seeds</strong> (pumpkin/sunflower; add chia/flax if desired)</li>
          <li>¾–1 cup <strong>whole-grain crunch</strong> (plain puffed rice, toasted oats, or unsweetened popcorn pieces)</li>
          <li>¾ cup <strong>dried fruit</strong> (raisins, cranberries, apricots chopped)</li>
          <li>Optional treat: ¼ cup <strong>dark chocolate</strong> pieces (≥60% cocoa)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Flavor add-ons (choose 1–2)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>½ tsp <strong>cinnamon</strong> or chai masala</li>
          <li>1 tsp <strong>cocoa powder</strong> (unsweetened)</li>
          <li>2 tbsp <strong>roasted coconut</strong> flakes (unsweetened)</li>
          <li>Zest of ½ <strong>orange/lemon</strong> (dry thoroughly; skip if reflux)</li>
        </ul>
        <p class="small">Low-sodium: choose <em>unsalted</em> nuts/seeds and rinse/dry any salted ones in a sieve if needed.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Steps -->
  <section id="steps" class="section">
    <h2>Step-by-step</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Mix (5–8 min)</p>
        <ol class="list">
          <li>In a large bowl, combine nuts and seeds. Add whole-grain crunch, then dried fruit and chocolate (if using).</li>
          <li>Sprinkle any spices; toss gently to distribute.</li>
          <li>Portion into 8–10 small airtight containers or zip bags.</li>
        </ol>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        <strong>Serving size:</strong> ~½ cup (about a small handful). Pair with water, tea, or a piece of fruit/curd for a mini-meal.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Ratios -->
  <section id="ratios" class="section">
    <h2>Smart ratios & portioning</h2>
    <div class="three">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Balanced energy</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>40%</strong> nuts • <strong>25%</strong> seeds • <strong>20%</strong> whole-grain • <strong>15%</strong> dried fruit</li>
          <li>Optional: ≤5% dark chocolate</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Budget / pantry</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Peanuts + sunflower seeds are affordable</li>
          <li>Use chopped dates/raisins instead of pricey berries</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Chew-soft</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Lightly <strong>toast</strong> nuts to soften, then <strong>chop</strong> small</li>
          <li>Choose softer nuts (walnuts, cashews) and moist fruits (apricots)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Condition swaps -->
  <section id="condition-swaps" class="section">
    <h2>Swaps by condition</h2>
    <div class="three">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Diabetes (Type 2)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Keep dried fruit to <strong>1 tbsp</strong> per ½-cup serving.</li>
          <li>Boost seeds/nuts; add cinnamon for sweetness without sugar.</li>
          <li>Pair with plain curd/unsweetened tea for steadier glucose.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Heart & BP</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Choose <strong>unsalted</strong> nuts; avoid candy-coated items.</li>
          <li>Favor walnuts/almonds + pumpkin seeds (healthy fats).</li>
          <li>Limit chocolate to a few pieces or skip.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">CKD (Kidney)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Nuts/seeds are potassium/phosphorus-rich—<strong>limit to ¼–⅓ cup</strong> per day if advised.</li>
          <li>Reduce dried fruit; skip coconut if saturated fat is a concern.</li>
          <li>Discuss individual targets with your clinician/dietitian.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Reflux (GERD)</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Skip citrus zest and strong mint if triggering.</li>
          <li>Small portions; chew well, sip water.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card ok">
        <strong>Allergy note:</strong> If nut allergy, make a <em>seed-only</em> mix (pumpkin, sunflower, chia, flax) + whole-grain crunch and dried fruit.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Make-ahead -->
  <section id="make-ahead" class="section">
    <h2>Make-ahead & storage</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Prep once</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Mix a monthly batch; store in an airtight jar in a cool, dark cupboard (2–4 weeks).</li>
          <li>In hot/humid weather, refrigerate or freeze to keep nuts fresh.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Portion control</p>
        <ul class="list">
          <li>Pre-portion into ½-cup snack bags/boxes to avoid handful creep.</li>
          <li>Label with date; use the oldest first.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- Tips -->
  <section id="tips" class="section">
    <h2>Texture & safety</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <ul class="list">
          <li><strong>Choking prevention:</strong> chop nuts; avoid large hard pieces. Sit upright; take small bites, sip water.</li>
          <li>Denture-friendly: choose softer nuts (walnuts/cashews), chop finely, add puffed grains to lighten crunch.</li>
          <li>Gas/comfort: increase seeds gradually; drink water through the day.</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div class="card info">
        Food safety: Keep hands/utensils dry; re-seal promptly. Avoid adding fresh fruit to stored mix—add at serving time.
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <!-- FAQ -->
  <section id="faq" class="section">
    <h2>Quick answers (FAQ)</h2>
    <div class="grid">
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Sweetness without sugar?</p>
        <p>Use cinnamon, cocoa, or a few chopped dates/raisins. Keep total dried fruit modest (1 tbsp per serving for diabetes).</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Salted nuts okay?</p>
        <p>Prefer unsalted. If using salted, mix half-and-half with unsalted and add extra seeds to dilute sodium.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">Nut allergy?</p>
        <p>Make a seed-only blend (pumpkin, sunflower, chia, flax) with puffed grains and dried fruit. Check labels for cross-contamination.</p>
      </div>
      <div class="card">
        <p class="kicker">How to toast?</p>
        <p>Dry-toast nuts/seeds in a pan over low heat 3–5 minutes, stirring until fragrant. Cool fully before mixing to keep crisp.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </section>
